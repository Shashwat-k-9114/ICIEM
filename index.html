About The Conference

BIT Mesra Noida Campus is organizing ICIEM 2026 with the theme "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" inspired by the G20 vision. This interdisciplinary conference aims to foster dialogue and innovation across Management, Applied Sciences, Computer Science, Electronics, Animation, and Multimedia.

The conference will provide a platform for knowledge exchange through Doctoral Research Paper Presentations, Student Research Competitions, and Case Study Showcases.

About BIT Mesra

Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra (BIT Mesra), a Deemed to be University, is a premier technical and research institution in Ranchi, Jharkhand. Established in 1955 by B.M. Birla, it is renowned for excellence in engineering, technology, management, and applied sciences.

The 780-acre campus offers state-of-the-art infrastructure and research facilities. BIT Mesra provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

About BIT Noida

Birla Institute of Technology, Noida (BIT Noida), an off-campus centre of BIT Mesra, is a distinguished institution known for academic excellence and professional education in the National Capital Region.

The campus boasts modern infrastructure, industry-aligned curriculum, and experienced faculty committed to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and leadership.

Call for Papers

BIT Mesra, Noida Campus invites academicians, industry professionals, research scholars, and students to submit original research papers for the International Conference on Innovation in Engineering & Management (ICIEM-2026) on the theme "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam".

We welcome cutting-edge contributions that reflect the spirit of unity and innovation for a sustainable future.


Who can participate?

Educators and Researchers from all disciplines, industry functionaries, Research Scholars and Students.
